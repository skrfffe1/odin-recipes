Pork Adobo



Description

Adobo or adobar is the immersion of cooked food in a stock composed variously of paprika, oregano, salt, garlic, and vinegar to preserve and enhance its flavor. The Portuguese variant is known as Carne de vinha

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless pork roast, cubed
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • ½ cup white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tablespoon tapioca starch
